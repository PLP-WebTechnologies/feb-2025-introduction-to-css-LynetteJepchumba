About Our Farm

We started this farm to promote sustainable food production and provide fresh catfish and trout directly from Nandi County. Our mission is to create employment, support healthier eating habits, and make use of our family land in a way that benefits the local community.

Why Choose Our Fish?

  • High-quality catfish and trout.
  • Farmed sustainably with care for the environment.
  • Delivered fresh to your doorstep.
  • Supporting local farmers and community growth.
